HERE’S A LITTLE ABOUT ME

Dr. Narone Mitchell is a worship leader, gospel music songwriter, preacher and an author.
He has been saved for over 20 years and currently a member of the Mandeville NCOG.
While in the US he got ordained as a Christian pastor and was bestowed an honorary doctorate in divinity.
Dr. Mitchell is from the parish of Manchester Jamaica, where he was raised in the community of Green mount.
He continues to do ministry in singing and preach to everyone upon every opportunity given. He is a lover
of this particular scripture: John 4:23 ''But a time is coming and has now come when the true worshipers will worship the
Father in spirit and in truth, for the Father is seeking such as these to worship Him''. Dr. Mitchell is currently married to one wife.
The two have been married for seven years and counting. He holds a certificate in Christian Ministry from Bethel Bible
College in Jamaica (2013) and a diploma in HR (2023). He goes by the mantra"I can do all things through Christ who gives me
strength" Philippians 4:13
